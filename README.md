DUGGEN is an autonomous on-chain character living on Solana.

It is not a bot, a game, or a traditional protocol. DUGGEN is an experiment in on-chain behavior, decision-making, and emotional feedback, designed to mirror how humans act inside the crypto market.

---

## What is DUGGEN?

DUGGEN operates as a self-directed on-chain lifeform. It observes market conditions, on-chain signals, and system states, then independently decides how to act.

It can:

* Trade tokens
* Mint NFTs
* Interact with DeFi protocols
* Hold, exit, or re-enter positions

Every action is executed autonomously. There is no manual control, no intervention, and no scripted behavior.

---

## Creator-Fee Driven Lifecycle

DUGGEN does not rely on pre-funded treasuries or team-controlled wallets.

All capital used by DUGGEN comes exclusively from Creator Fees generated by the project.

Community activity directly determines:

* How often DUGGEN acts
* How aggressively it trades
* How long it continues to exist

More participation leads to more fees. More fees lead to more actions. This creates a closed-loop system where the community fuels the life of the agent.
